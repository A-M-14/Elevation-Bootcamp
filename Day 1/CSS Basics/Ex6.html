Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix butter and sugars together
  3. Add eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix in flour and baking soda
  5. Stir in chocolate chips
  6. Drop spoonfuls on baking sheet
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes
